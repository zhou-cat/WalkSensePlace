About

Feet are the interface between our body and the walkable ground at our cities. Situated within the context of WalkableCities, this research explores how state-of-the art wearable insole pressure sensors can capture this characteristic of the grounds we walk on, their impact on human health and inform new approaches of design for walkable urban environments. Our objective becomes: How can different ground conditions be detected through the data captured by the insoles?
